Chiang Mai has one of the most active expat social scenes in Southeast Asia. Whatever you are here for, however long you are staying, and whatever you are into, there is a weekly event for you. The list below is updated and verified. If something is wrong or no longer running, let us know.

For the best weekly email roundup of events: subscribe to Steve's Newsletter. General community discussion: Chiang Mai Events Facebook Group.
