Expat Breakfast Club

Chiang Mai Expats Club Monthly Breakfast

The Chiang Mai Expat Breakfast Club is a big part of the monthly calendar of events of the CEC! It happens on the first Saturday of the month, with the general meeting happening on the 4th Saturday, every other month.

The Monthly Breakfast is open for ALL and you can RSVP to attend. Expats come in many variations including retirees, digital nomads, teachers, inhabitants of Chiang Mai, tourists and anyone else who wants to come along. You do not have to be a member of the CEC club, but there are benefits to joining, including 50 baht off the full breakfast option.

Location of the Expat Breakfast Club

Usually around 100 people meet together at The Duke’s Ruam Chok branch! The Expat Breakfast Club has been meeting at the The Duke’s since COVID and it’s just a great place to enjoy breakfast and the company of friends.

What happens at the Expat Breakfast Club?

It’s just a meeting about friends, no, food, no, friends, no, food, ok, it’s a meeting about friends and food. It’s great to go along and make some new friends as there are always new faces and many old faces. The Expat Breakfast Club is a chance for newcomers to ask questions, to get connected and to enjoy great food.

 The Food Options

A delicious full breakfast – with many healthy choices,  345฿ (members), 345฿ (non-members – normally 445฿)

The full breakfast is just incredible! All you favorite western breakfast options and more.

Its another great thing about living in Chiang Mai.
