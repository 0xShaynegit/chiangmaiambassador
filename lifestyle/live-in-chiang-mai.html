Live in Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai is a fantastic place to visit, but to live in Chiang Mai is so much better. Why? Well there are just so many reasons -

The lifestyle, Chiang Mai is know for being an easy going place with most of the modern creature comforts, just around the corner and everything else available, even a little bit further away. In most cases, you do’t even need to leave your house if you don’t want to.

The weather in Chiang Mai is great most of the time, with usually a week of cold(10 degrees Celsius. 40-50s F), a month of smog(at an above average level) and the rest of the year being 30- high 30s Celcius and around 80s+ F), June to October are the rainy season with similar temperatures, but a lot more rain.

The people of Chiang Mai are amazing, their willingness to look after and help you in anyway they can is legendary. They love to host the world in Chiang Mai and make everyone feel most at home. Sometimes too much at home and the tourists just never want to leave. Yes, that’s probably why you are reading this. You have visited, but you don’t want to visit any more.

There is a fantastic resource for people wanting to come and live here long term, it’s called LIVING IN CHIANG MAI . Well that was easy! Or if you are looking to retire in Chiang Mai is your best option.
