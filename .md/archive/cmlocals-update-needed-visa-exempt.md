## What Changed
Thailand's Cabinet approved **May 19, 2026** to END the 60-day visa exemption scheme (which ran July 2024 - May 2026).

**New Rules (effective 15 days after Royal Gazette publication):**
- Visa-free stays return to pre-2024 standard: 30 days (most countries), 15 days (some countries)
- Land border entries capped at 2 per calendar year
- Air arrivals may have different rules than land border crossings (TBA in Royal Gazette)
