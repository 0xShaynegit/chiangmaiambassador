TDAC Thailand Digital Arrival Card

Before You Travel

The Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC) is a newly introduced online system designed to streamline the entry process into Thailand. Travelers must complete it within three days of their proposed arrival. This digital form replaces the traditional paper arrival card and can be filled out in advance, aiming to make immigration procedures quicker, more efficient, and eco-friendly by cutting down on paper use.

THERE IS NO COST AND IT’S A 5 MINUTE JOB!

BE CAREFUL WHICH WEBSITE YOU USE!

Important Warning

Always use the official Government website at https://tdac.immigration.go.th/ when submitting your digital arrival card. Beware of unofficial websites that may charge fees—the TDAC service is completely free and there is never a charge to complete this form.

Mobile or Desktop Version?

On your mobile, many travelers trying the TDAC for the first time may find it a bit of an adventure. In my personal experience, completing the TDAC was challenging on a mobile device. I was actually on a bus heading to the border to briefly leave Thailand and return shortly after, so I needed to complete the form as quickly as possible. The system required typing at least three letters in many fields before options would appear.

While this seemed straightforward, I struggled to spell “MUEANG” correctly so it would show up in the dropdown menu. This difficulty prompted me to switch to the desktop version on my mobile device, hoping for a smoother process. Unfortunately, switching to desktop mid-way caused the form to crash, forcing me to start over from scratch.

Mobile Dropdown Menus

Another major frustration was with the dropdown menus on mobile, where the keyboard sometimes didn’t appear. This issue made it impossible to scroll through or search within the dropdown options, adding to the clunky experience. Despite these hurdles, after about 20 minutes and some patience, I was able to complete the form, download the PDF receipt, and receive a confirmation email.

Warning Reminder

Do not pay any website or third party to fill out your TDAC. The official site is operated by Thailand’s Immigration Bureau and is free to use. Using unofficial services may expose you to scams or unnecessary charges.

Completion Timeframe

You only have 3 days to select your entry day, so you can’t fill it out too early!
Though the TDAC platform currently feels rough around the edges, especially on mobile devices, it marks a positive step toward modernizing Thailand’s entry process. Once the learning curve is overcome, the system offers promise for a smoother, paperless experience.

TDAC Health Declaration

You just need to explain the last few countries you have been to, if any. As I was doing a border run, I was only out of Thailand a few minutes, so I entered Thailand on the form.

WE HAVE GOT TO GET USED TO IT.

TDAC is the new way of entering Thailand!

In conclusion, travelers preparing to visit Thailand and needing to complete the TDAC should allow extra time and patience, and consider using a desktop device when possible. While the process can feel a bit cumbersome at first, it represents an important advancement toward improving the efficiency of border controls and enhancing convenience for travelers. It will be interesting to see how this system develops and improves with feedback from users in the coming months.
