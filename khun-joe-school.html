Khun Joe School and Beauty Salon

Khun Joe School and Beauty Salon

Your Gateway to Hairdressing and Beauty Training Excellence in Chiang Mai

Khun Joe School is a popular place for hair and beauty training in Chiang Mai. The school started in 1975 and now teaches more than 2,000 students each year. It is found on the north side of the moat around the Old City, right across from Jok Sompet Market. You can walk in for a free haircut between 9am and 3pm. Most of the haircuts are done by students, with trainers watching closely. The cuts are not perfect, but always fair. It works well for both men and women. As a guy, I feel respected every time I visit. Sometimes you have to wait, especially when the place is busy.

Exceptional Training, Lasting Skills

Khun Joe School offers courses approved by the Ministry of Education. There are more than 20 branches across Thailand and over 200 instructors on the team. You find both short and long courses. The topics cover basic haircuts, modern styles, coloring, and other beauty treatments.

When you finish your course, you receive a diploma that you can use to work in salons or teach others. Knowing some Thai will help with communication, but it is not required. Tips are always welcome, and students appreciate them. Most trainers can speak basic English so you can get by without much Thai. The school does not require you to book ahead. You just show up.

A Real-World Learning Environment

For many locals and expats, a highlight of Khun Joe School is its walk-in, free haircuts available Monday to Friday, 9am-3pm. The school operates as both a learning ground for students and a community service hub. As a client, the experience is refreshingly honest and you’ll often be helped by a student supervised by a trainer. Most regulars, including myself, agree that you “walk in with hair and out with a reasonable haircut for free.” While results may vary as students hone their craft, the atmosphere is friendly, and tips for students are always appreciated.

Despite being known for its stylish female haircuts, men are welcome and treated with respect. I’ve personally seen a mix of trainees, young, enthusiastic stylists and seasoned instructors who work together to make clients feel comfortable, regardless of age or gender. Limited Thai language skills are helpful but not essential, especially if you’re easygoing about small imperfections. Communication, patience, and mutual smiles go a long way.

It works for Me and the Trainees

I first heard about Khun Joe School in 2014, but never visited for a haircut until recently when my barber was unavailable. I have returned several times. I have had my hair done by both young men and older ladies. Most days, the same young stylist seems to be working, with trainers helping or finishing up the cut. My style is simple, and the school suits my needs.

Students receive real-world experience, guidance from caring trainers, and the satisfaction of helping real clients. Graduates report feeling “confident and prepared” for professional opportunities. The school’s social media and website feature photos of classes, graduates, and events, giving a sense of vibrant community spirit.

Contact and Location

You can find Khun Joe School at 128/1, Chang Puak Market, Manee Nopparat Road, Sriphum, Chiang Mai. The phone number is 053-210763 and mobile is 089-8119065. The atmosphere is busy but friendly, and the service is reliable. Each visit means you support local students and get practical results. The school provides hands-on training and recognized qualifications, giving people skills for life in the hair and beauty industry.
