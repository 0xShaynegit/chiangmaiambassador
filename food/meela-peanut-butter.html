Amazing All Natural Peanut Butter Made in Chiang Mai

Meela Peanut Butter

After eating Meela Peanut Butter for the last 8 years, I can clearly state that this is the best peanut butter in Chiang Mai! This peanut butter is made from just peanuts and a pinch of salt.

Meela peanut butter, found notably in Chiang Mai, is praised for being a pure and simple peanut butter made from just peanuts and a touch of salt. This minimalist approach means it retains the natural qualities of peanuts without unnecessary additives, aligning with health-conscious consumption trends. Generally, peanut butter like Meela’s is rich in protein (about 8g per 2 tbsp), healthy fats, mainly monounsaturated fats, and provides essential minerals like potassium and magnesium. It contains about 200 calories per 2 tablespoons, making it energy-dense but also satisfying. Meela peanut butter’s clean ingredient list can be especially appealing for those seeking a natural, unrefined spread with benefits such as plant-based protein, fiber, and antioxidant minerals.

Recipes with peanut butter range from healthful to indulgent. Healthy options include no bake energy balls, peanut butter-banana protein muffins, and overnight oats with powdered peanut butter.these recipes emphasize whole food ingredients, moderate added sugars, and fiber-rich components. On the less healthy side, recipes like peanut butter rice krispie treats or peanut butter cups use more sugar, oils, and processed ingredients, resulting in higher calorie, less nutrient-dense snacks.

In summary, Meela peanut butter stands out as a natural, nutritious choice ideal for a variety of recipes, from wholesome snacks to indulgent treats.
