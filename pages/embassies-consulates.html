Important Notes on Consular Services

  • Always Call Ahead. Contact information changes frequently. Phone before visiting to confirm office hours and services.
  • Verify Official Information. Check official government websites for the most current details on visas, documents required, and service fees.
  • Bring Correct Documents. Each consulate has specific requirements. Arrive prepared with necessary documentation to avoid wasted trips.
  • Keep Records Updated. If you find any errors in this directory, contact Chiang Mai Ambassador to help keep this resource current for others.
