The BEST Chiang Mai Resource Links

Chiang Mai is an awesome place and you will come to understand that very quickly. These best Chiang Mai resource links will give you lots of great information about Chiang Mai and will get you connected to the people that will help you in daily life. Slowly I will be sectioning of this article and you will be able to find the information in more categorized and structured layout. The page was created as a resource for all the good local knowledge and groups to find accurate information. Thanks for reading and I hope you enjoy.

There are so many social groups, the nomad groups, the business groups, the teacher groups and all the rest of the groups you can become a part of. You can be as unsocial or as social as you like. Basic Accommodations Map 

About this map, these are good areas to move into if you are staying longer term and especially if you are more of a digital nomad type. The black circle is Santitham, my area, and monthly accommodation is between 3000-7000 baht. The green circle is Jet Yod, around monthly accommodation is between 4000-12000 baht. The red circle is Nimman, around monthly accommodation is between 5000-15000 baht.

*** You may get cheaper or more expensive but these are averages. I suspect there are over 400 accommodation places in just those 3 circles, so get on a bike and find somewhere you like.
