Chiang Mai Hub

Everything you need to know about the Rose of the North. From neighbourhoods and cost of living to culture and activities.

Chiang Mai is a city of contrasts. Old temples and modern cafes. Mountain scenery and urban energy. Deep roots in Thai tradition and an open welcome to newcomers from around the world. Whether you're planning to stay for weeks or build a life here, this hub connects you to everything you need to understand the city.
